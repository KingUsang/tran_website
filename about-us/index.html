About Us

The Root Access Network (TRAN) is a cybersecurity education company dedicated to making digital safety knowledge accessible to everyone. We design and deliver innovative cybersecurity learning products and services, including educational board games, digital learning tools, training programs, workshops, and curriculum materials for schools, organisations, and communities.

Join the Network Watch Our Videos

Our Story

TRAN began as a community helping people grow in technology through mentorship and shared learning. As we grew, we discovered a bigger gap — people were using the internet without understanding how to stay safe. This led us to evolve into a cybersecurity education company, building simple and practical learning tools like CYTRAC. Today, we empower individuals, schools, and organisations with the knowledge to navigate the digital world safely.

Our Mission

To build a world where individuals develop strong cybersecurity awareness from an early age, and organisations foster responsible digital citizenship.
